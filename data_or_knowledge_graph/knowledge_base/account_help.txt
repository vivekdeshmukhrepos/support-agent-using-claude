ACCOUNT HELP AND SUPPORT

1. Password Reset
If you forgot your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and you'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes. Click the link and create a new password. Your password must be at least 8 characters long and contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

2. Account Verification
New accounts need to be verified before use. Check your email for a verification link. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or request a new verification email from the account settings page.

3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We recommend enabling 2FA for security. Go to Settings > Security > Enable 2FA. You can use Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or receive codes via SMS.

4. Account Deactivation
To deactivate your account, go to Settings > Account > Deactivate Account. Your data will be retained for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can reactivate within this period by logging in.

5. Updating Profile Information
Visit Settings > Profile to update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture. All changes are saved immediately.

6. Login Issues
If you can't log in:
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try using a different browser
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Ensure you're using the correct email address
- Wait 15 minutes if you've tried logging in too many times (account locked temporarily)
