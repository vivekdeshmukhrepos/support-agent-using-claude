BILLING AND PAYMENT FAQ

1. Billing Cycles
Our billing cycle runs from the 1st to the last day of each month. Your invoice is generated on the first day of the following month. Charges appear on your credit card statement 1-2 business days after the invoice date.

2. Accepted Payment Methods
We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (ACH in the US)

3. Invoice and Receipts
You can download invoices from Billing > Invoice History. Each invoice shows itemized charges, applicable taxes, and payment method used. Invoices are emailed automatically on the first day of each month.

4. Subscription Plans
We offer three plans:
- Basic: $29/month - 100 API calls, 1GB storage
- Pro: $99/month - 10,000 API calls, 100GB storage
- Enterprise: Custom pricing - unlimited API calls, dedicated support

Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Pro-rata refunds are issued for upgrades.

5. Refunds and Cancellation
Cancellations take effect immediately. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

6. Failed Payment
If a payment fails, you'll receive an email notification. You have 5 days to update your payment method. If not updated, your account will be suspended.

7. Taxes
Applicable sales tax will be added based on your billing address. Tax-exempt organizations can provide a tax certificate for exemption.

8. Credit Card Security
We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. Your card details are never stored on our servers. We use tokenization for recurring charges.
